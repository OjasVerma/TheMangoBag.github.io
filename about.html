About Us

We , The Mango Bag, believe in the contribution of Community , for Humanity.


Even a minute concern towards our mother nature can cause a change in betterment of our precious ecosystem , keeping that as our sole philosophy , we thrive to germinate a environmentalist in the younger generations so that they can be developed as better citizens.


So if you want to do a little towards this earth , join us and witness the change

A Little Activity For A Major Change

It's peak summers going on and no one of us can imagine it without mangoes , isn't it ? So next time you relish this King of Fruits , make sure you save its seed.


You can plant that seed in your garden , pot or anywhere you like , and carefully water it on a daily basis.


By doing so you can be responsible to give this nature one more chance to replenish.
